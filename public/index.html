How to Play ▾
1

Create a room — choose your grid size and click Create Room.

2

Share the link — copy and send your unique room URL to a friend.

3

Take turns — draw one line per turn between two adjacent dots.

4

Claim boxes — complete the 4th side of a box to score a point and go again.

5

Win — the player with the most boxes when the board is full wins!
